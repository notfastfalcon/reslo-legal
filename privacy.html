Privacy Policy

Alpha Release · Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Reslo ("Reslo", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use the Reslo mobile application.

Alpha Testing Notice: Reslo is currently an alpha release. Features, functionality, and data may change, be modified, or be removed at any time. During testing, some user data may be reset or deleted.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth
  • City
  • Gender
  • Profile photo
  • Photos and media you upload
  • Restaurant ratings, reviews, recommendations, and visit history
  • Social connections and interactions within the app

Information collected automatically:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device and operating system information
  • App usage and analytics data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • Approximate or precise location information (with your permission)

Information from sign-in providers:

  • Apple Sign In information
  • Google Sign In information

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify eligibility requirements
  • Provide and improve Reslo features
  • Enable restaurant discovery and social functionality
  • Personalize your experience
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Respond to support requests
  • Protect the security of the platform
  • Communicate important service updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers that help operate Reslo, including:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, and file storage)
  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Sign In

Your profile information, reviews, ratings, photos, and other content may be visible to other users as part of the app's social features.

We may disclose information when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of Reslo and its users.

4. Data Storage

Your information may be stored and processed in Canada, the United States, or other countries where our service providers operate.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Because this is an alpha release, testing data may be modified, migrated, reset, or deleted without notice.

6. Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

You may also withdraw consent for certain processing activities, subject to legal or operational requirements.

8. Age Requirement

Reslo is intended only for individuals who are at least sixteen (16) years old.

By using the app, you represent and warrant that you are at least 16 years of age.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a person under 16, we may suspend the account and delete the associated information.
