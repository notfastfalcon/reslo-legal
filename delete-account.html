Delete Your Reslo Account

App: Reslo · Developer: arsh.narkhede.projectx@gmail.com

This page explains how to delete your Reslo account and what happens to your data, in line with Google Play and Apple requirements.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Reslo and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account is deactivated immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email arsh.narkhede.projectx@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process it within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When deletion completes, the following are permanently removed:

  • Your profile and personal information (name, email, date of birth, city, gender, phone, bio, profile photo)
  • Your reviews, ratings, and dish ratings
  • Your recommendations (sent and received)
  • Your connections and friend requests
  • Wishlists you own, and your membership in shared wishlists
  • Planned outings you created, and your participation, attendance, and votes
  • Your notifications
  • Your authentication record (account login)

What may be retained

  • Grace period: Your account is held in a deactivated state for 30 days. Signing back in within 30 days cancels the deletion and restores your account. After 30 days, all data above is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • Shared group activity: Group outings or visits that included other people remain visible to those other participants, but your personal participation is removed and you are no longer identified.
  • Legal/operational: Limited records may be retained only as required to comply with legal obligations or prevent fraud/abuse, then deleted.
Because Reslo is currently in alpha, account and test data may also be reset or removed during the testing period.
